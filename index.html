Information about Ferrero Rocher

Ferrero Rocher is a delicious chocolate knowed by his unique flavor. It features a whole roasted hazelnut encased in a thin wafer shell filled with hazelnut chocolate and covered in milk chocolate and chopped hazelnuts.

Interesting Facts

  • Ferrero Rocher was created in 1982 in a small Italian city called Alba.
  • Each Ferrero Rocher piece is wrapped in gold foil, making it look luxurious and special.
  • The chocolate is popular worldwide and is often given as a gift during festive seasons. Everyone loves it!
